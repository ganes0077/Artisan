Ordering & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are securely processed via Stripe, ensuring your data is protected.
Can I modify or cancel my order after it has been placed?
Due to the nature of handcrafted goods, modifications or cancellations are only possible within **2 hours** of placing the order, provided the artisan has not yet begun production or packaging. Please contact support@artisan.com immediately.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?
As products are shipped directly from the artisan's studio, delivery times vary. Standard shipping usually takes 5-10 business days domestically and 10-20 days internationally. You will receive a tracking number once the item has shipped.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship worldwide! International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or brokerage fees levied by their country.
